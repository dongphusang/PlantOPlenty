	A fully-grown areca palm houseplant is quite expensive, so they are suaully purchased as small, tabletop plants.
	
	They add six to ten inches (15-25 cm) of growth per year until they reach as mature height of 6 or 7 feet (1.8-2.1 m).

	Areca palm is one of the few palms that can tolerate trimming without serious harm, making it possible to keep mature plants indoors for their full lifespan of up to ten years.
